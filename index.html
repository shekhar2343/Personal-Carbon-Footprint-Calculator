Sustainability Tips

Small changes that can add up over a year

Transport

Carpool, switch one commute to public transit, or replace short drives with cycling.

Home Energy

Use efficient appliances, unplug idle devices, and shift to cleaner electricity when available.

Food

Reducing red meat and food waste can significantly lower your footprint over time.

Consumption

Buy durable products, repair more often, and choose second-hand where practical.
